How It Works

1. Submit Your Info

Fill out a quick form with your NYSC details, skills, and preferred type of work.

2. We Search for PPAs

We manually search for suitable Place of Primary Assignment (PPA) based on your info.

3. You Get Matched

Once a match is found, we notify you with the details and steps to proceed.

4. Start at Your PPA

Take your referral letter to the PPA and begin your primary assignment stress-free.
